Demonstration of Complex Exponentials

The graph below is showing the real part of the complex exponential function y(t) = e[(λ + μi)t].

The top slider controls λ, while the bottom slider controls μ— try playing around with them and see what effect each has on the shape of the graph.

You can also click on "Imaginary Part" to see the imaginary part of e[(λ + μi)t]
