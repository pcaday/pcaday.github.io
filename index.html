Currently, I am a Simons Postdoctoral Fellow in Rice University's Computational and Applied Mathematics department and a member of the Geo-Mathematical Imaging Group, led by Maarten V. de Hoop.

My research focuses on the development of numerical algorithms for wave-based imaging problems (e.g., seismic and ultrasound imaging) backed by rigorous mathematical analysis.
